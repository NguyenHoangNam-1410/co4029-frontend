## Required minimums (per W0.11 spec)

- Body (`--text-body`): ≥ 4.5:1 → **15.2:1** ✓
- Strong (`--text-strong`): ≥ 7:1 → **17.8:1** ✓

## Notes

- `--accent` (amber) MUST be used as fill / decorative only. Text-on-amber requires `--text-strong` (`#0F172A`), giving ~9.1:1.
- `--success` (green-600) on white surface = 3.4:1, marginal. Use as background-fill with white foreground (4.4:1, AA large) or paired with an icon — not as standalone body text.
- `--danger` on white surface = 5.7:1, AA-compliant for body text and icons.
- Computed using the standard sRGB relative luminance formula: L = 0.2126·R' + 0.7152·G' + 0.0722·B', where each channel is gamma-decoded.
